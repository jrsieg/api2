Is it safe to run outside?

          How it Works

          RunChecker sifts through weather data in your identified location. The Air Quality Index (AQI) is a reading of air particulate ranging from 0 to 500. An AQI reading of 0 to 50 represents good quality, 50 to 300 represents fair to poor quality, and 300 to 500 is hazardous. Knowing the safety of outdoor exercise is now just a click away with RunChecker!
